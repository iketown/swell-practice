# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The Swell's working band members and two administrators. Members usually arrive from a phone or laptop to answer one question quickly: what parts do I need to learn for this lineup? Administrators maintain the song library, member roster, bands, and song-by-song assignments while planning rehearsals and substitute configurations.

## Product Purpose

The Swell Parts is a small internal parts-distribution tool. It keeps rehearsal assets organized by song and part, records each member's normal song assignments once, and stores only the exceptions for a particular band. Success means a member can open their page, choose a band, and immediately understand their work, while an admin can spot uncovered parts and make lineup changes without rebuilding every assignment.

## Brand Personality

Practical, warm, confident. The interface should feel like a dependable working set list: friendly enough for a close-knit band, compact enough for real planning, and clear under time pressure.

## Anti-references

Avoid a generic SaaS analytics dashboard, a decorative music-festival aesthetic, or a dense enterprise staffing system. Do not turn the assignment workflow into a spreadsheet full of repeated records or hide coverage behind ornamental cards.

## Design Principles

1. Show the effective answer, then explain whether it came from a default or override.
2. Store and display exceptions as exceptions; unchanged defaults should require no maintenance.
3. Make missing coverage visually unmistakable without relying on color alone.
4. Keep the member experience calmer and simpler than the admin experience.
5. Prefer direct manipulation for assigning parts, with equally clear keyboard and touch alternatives.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Maintain keyboard-accessible assignment controls, visible focus, semantic labels, 44-pixel touch targets where practical, reduced-motion compatibility, and text or icon cues in addition to color for default, override, and unassigned states.
